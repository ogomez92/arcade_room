      How to play

      Move your mallet with the Arrow keys, a gamepad stick, or by dragging on a touch screen. You are confined to your half.

      There is no hit button. Drive your mallet through the puck to add pace. Aim is pure physics — you learn shot lines by ear.

      Up-table is in front of you; your goal is behind. The puck hisses; a ping marks the opponent goal; a rising alarm warns when a shot is heading for your net.

      Status keys: F1 score, F2 puck bearing, F3 your position, F4 match state. Esc leaves the match.
