How to play

Six critters live around you. They poke their heads out of their lairs and you must whack them with the rubber hammer before they hide again.

Lair layout

  • Q — front-left lair
  • E — straight ahead
  • T — front-right lair
  • Z — back-left lair
  • C — directly behind
  • B — back-right lair

You stand on D in the centre. D is empty — never whacked. Each lair has its own creature with its own voice, so you can tell them apart by sound.

Scoring

  • Hit early for more points. A fresh popup is worth up to 150; a dying popup is worth as little as 30.
  • If a critter hides without being hit, you lose one miss. Five misses ends the run.
  • The longer you survive, the faster the critters pop and the more pop at once. The music layers up too.

Status keys

  • F1 — read score
  • F2 — read misses left
  • F3 — read current level
  • Esc — back to the menu

Learn the critters

Click each button to hear that critter pop out of its lair from your point of view at the centre.
