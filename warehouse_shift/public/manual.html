Warehouse Shift

Audio-first Sokoban in a modern synth warehouse.

Goal

Push every crate onto a goal pad. A crate can only be pushed, never pulled, so every move should be planned before you commit.

Audio Model

The listener is screen-locked: north is always in front, east is right, south is behind, and west is left. The ambience is a procedural warehouse rumble: floor vibration, ventilation, distant compressors, pallet shifts, and soft electrical hum evolve as time passes. The current target pings periodically. Crates use darker tones, goals ring brighter, stepping onto a goal announces it, and blocked movement sounds from the blocked tile.

Progress

The campaign has twenty curated levels. Solving a level unlocks the next one. Best records are saved per level and compare moves first, pushes second, undos third, and time last.
