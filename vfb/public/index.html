How to Play

You pilot the Solvalou, an endlessly forward-scrolling ship. Aerial enemies must be shot with the zapper beam. Ground targets are destroyed with bombs. Every three levels you face a Genesis mothership boss. Game is endless: try to reach as far as you can.

Combat keys

  • Z — Fire zapper beam (hold to fire continuously)
  • X — Drop bomb (hold to drop continuously)
  • C — Anti-aircraft burst (clears all aerial enemies; finite supply)
  • A — Spend 3 shieldbits for an instant clearing beam

Movement

  • ← / → — Strafe left / right (lateral position 0–10)
  • ↑ — Speed up (faster forward scroll)
  • ↓ — Slow down

Game flow

  • Space — Request store at end of level (level 4+, costs 15 credits)
  • P — Pause / resume
  • Esc — Quit current run, return to menu
  • Q — Rage quit (immediate game over)

Status announcements (spoken via screen reader)

  • S — Speak current score
  • L — Speak lives remaining
  • E — Speak current level
  • B — Speak bursts remaining
  • D — Speak shieldbits remaining
  • M — Speak credits
  • V — Speak level progress (percent of level traveled)

Scoring & progression

  • Aerial enemies: more points the further away you kill them.
  • Combo: chain kills within 1.5 seconds for bonus points and credits.
  • Avoiding enemy shots gives a small bonus.
  • Extra lives: at 20,000 points, then 60,000, 120,000, …
  • Towers (when you hear the alarm, drop a bomb immediately): destroying a tower sets a checkpoint and gives a hefty score.
  • Items (from scorpion ships): rapid fire, bomb area, anti-aircraft burst, beam velocity, shieldbits.
  • Genesis mothership (every 3 levels): don’t pass it—its forcefield kills you and clears your shields.

Store (after every Genesis kill, or on request)

  • Permanent upgrades to fire rate, beam velocity, bomb area, powerup duration.
  • Per-session limits: up to 3 lives, 5 shieldbits, 3 bursts.

Use headphones for the best stereo positioning of enemies and items.
