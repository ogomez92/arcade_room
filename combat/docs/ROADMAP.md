# Roadmap

## v2.0 — Shipped in this branch
- Five mechs with unique stats, primary/secondary weapons, and mobility options.
- Full weapon set: pistol, machine gun, shotgun, rail cannon, homing missile, disruptor beam, melee, ram boost.
- Enclosed 400×400 m arena with wall-collision damage and mech-vs-mech ramming (including fall-attack multiplier).
- Parking-aid wall radar with directional pitch buckets and stereo panning.
- Aim sonar with primary/secondary range switching (Shift+F / Shift+R).
- Spatialized persistent engine sounds and footsteps for both mechs.
- Offline AI opponent with aim, strafe, weapon selection, and jump/jetpack behavior.
- Online 1v1 via PeerJS (room code based).
- Screen-reader friendly menus, live-region announcements, and a dedicated "Learn game sounds" screen.
- Pause / resume / rematch.

## v2.1 — Polishing candidates
- Haptics for gamepad (rumble on damage, ramming, weapon fire).
- Configurable controls and key-rebinding UI.
- Mech-specific voice callouts ("Boost ready", "Missile locked").
- Additional arena sizes / obstacle layouts.
- Death-cam audio replay of the final seconds.

## v3.0 — Stretch
- More mech variants (flight, heavy artillery, stealth cloak).
- Full lobby/server matchmaking instead of manual room codes.
- Best-of-N matches with persistent health between rounds.
- Accessible replay files.
