Space Invaders!

Hold the line. Identify the ship by ear; pick the matching weapon; fire on the centred target.

How to Play

Controls

  • Left/Right — pan the crosshair across the stereo field.
  • Space — fire the current weapon.
  • 1/2/3 — switch weapon. Q/E cycle.
  • Esc — pause.

Status Hotkeys

  • F1 — score.
  • F2 — lives.
  • F3 — energy + wave.
  • F4 — next chain ship.
