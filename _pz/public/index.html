      How to Play

      Controls

      • Up / Down — accelerate / brake.
      • Left / Right — steer.
      • 1…9 — select a held pizza.
      • Space — flick the selected pizza.
      • Enter — auto-recenter the bike on the road.
      • Esc — pause.

      Status Hotkeys

      • F1 — repeat last GPS instruction.
      • F2 — held pizza details.
      • F3 — distance to the restaurant.
      • F4 — time and tip total.
      • F5 — your current street address.

      Objective

      Pick up pizzas at the shop, deliver each one to its address, and head back for the next job. The longer you take, the smaller the tip — and a $0 delivery ends the run. Mind traffic lights and pedestrians.
