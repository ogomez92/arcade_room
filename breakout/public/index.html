How to Play

  • Left/Right or A/D: move the paddle.
  • Space or Enter: launch the ball or release a caught ball.
  • Tab: announce the ball and paddle alignment.
  • The paddle is a low panned body tone, the ball is a moving rolling/air sound, and falling powerups have their own panned flutter.
  • Clear every brick. Catch falling powerups for wide paddle, slow ball, catch, laser, multiball, or extra life.
