# Audio Breakout GDD

Breakout playable by ear. The ball has a quiet physical locator, impacts carry material identity, and powerups add Arkanoid-style variety without masking ball tracking.

## Powerups

- Wide paddle: temporary wider paddle.
- Slow ball: temporary speed reduction.
- Catch: center paddle hits hold the ball until launch.
- Laser: timed automatic twin shots from the paddle.
- Multiball: splits the current ball into two extra balls.
- Extra life: immediate life gain.
