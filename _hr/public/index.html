    How to Play

    • You ride a horse against rivals on a 1-kilometre track.
    • Space — whip your horse to push it faster. Each whip costs stamina.
    • Up Arrow — jump.
    • Whip too much and stamina drops; the horse slows. Stop whipping to recover.
    • Other horses are heard around you.
    • F1 — current position.
    • F2 — your stamina and speed.
    • F3 — distance to the next jump.
    • F4 — distance remaining and elapsed time.
    • Score is built from finishing position, clean and perfect jumps, average stamina kept, average speed, and finish time. Crashes lose points.
