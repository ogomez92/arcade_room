BRAWL!

Audio melee. Top-down arena, fists and feet, gendered voices, no weapons.

Headphones strongly recommended.

Choose your fighter

Each fighter has a distinct voice and a default style.

    How to Play

    Movement

    • W A S D — walk around the arena.

    Attacks

    • T — high punch.
    • G — low punch.
    • U — high kick.
    • J — low sweep kick.

    Defense and mobility

    • . — block.
    • L — duck.
    • O — jump.

    Knockdowns

    When the opponent is on the ground:

    • High attacks miss completely.
    • Low attacks deal a heavy stomp bonus.
    • They rise after about a second and a half.

    Combos

    Land hits in sequence. Some openers:

    • T T G — One-Two-Body.
    • T G U — Combination.
    • G G J — Liver Crusher.
    • T G U J — Hurricane.

    Mounting and walk-on

    • Jump while next to a downed opponent to mount them.
    • While mounted, movement keys step on body parts.
    • Press jump again for a heavier slam.
    • You cannot punch or kick while mounted.

    When you are knocked down

    • You will not get up automatically while pinned.
    • Mash movement keys to throw the opponent off.

    Status keys

    • F1 — your health.
    • F2 — opponent health.
    • F3 — distance and direction to opponent.
    • F4 — current combo chain.
    • F5 — your posture and the opponent’s.
    • 0 — debug heal.
