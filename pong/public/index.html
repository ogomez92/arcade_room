How to play

  • Arrow keys — slide your paddle along your end of the table.
  • S — swing straight back.
  • A — swing and angle the ball to the left.
  • D — swing and angle the ball to the right.
  • When you serve, press A, S, or D to launch the ball. S goes straight; A and D angle it. You have a few seconds before the serve transfers to your opponent.
  • Timing. The ball rolls louder as it nears you. Swing while it is just in front of your paddle, or you will miss.
  • Powerups. A second ball rolls across your end now and then — let it touch your paddle to pick it up. Powerups include a wider paddle, a stronger swing, a freeze on your opponent, curve shots, a shield, and bouncy walls.
  • Audio map. The ball pans left and right as it moves across the table, and grows louder as it approaches your end. Your paddle clicks tell you where it is.
  • Multiplayer. When a team has more than one player, the active player rotates after every goal so a teammate takes over.
  • Escape — leave a single-player match and return to the menu.
