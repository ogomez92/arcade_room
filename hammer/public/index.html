    How to Play

    • Listen to the target pitch — a steady tone.
    • Then a slide sweeps from a low pitch up to a high pitch.
    • Press Space or Enter to SWING the hammer when the slide matches the target.
    • Score 100 to ring the bell! 50–99 the crowd cheers and you continue. Below 50 the run ends.
    • Each round the slide gets faster. Levels 1–2 use musical notes; level 3+ uses any pitch.
    • F1 read score · F2 level.
