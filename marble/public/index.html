How to play

Roll the marble to the exit without falling in a pit.

  • Tilt the board with the Arrow keys or WASD.
  • A soft pulsing tone marks the exit.
  • A low growl warns of the nearest pit.
  • Clear each level fast to score more.
  • You hear from the marble's point of view.
  • F1 status, F2 exit, F3 pit, F4 speed. Esc pauses.
