    How to Play
    
          
    
            
    Controls
    
            
      
              
    • Up/Down — switch bar (1–4).
      • 
              
    • Left/Right — walk along the bar.
      • 
              
    • Space at the kegs — hold to fill a mug, release to sling it right.
      • 
              
    • Esc — pause / quit.
      • 
              
    • F1 position, F2 score & lives, F3 nearest customer, F4 empty mugs.
      • 
            
    
            
    Tending the Bar
    
            
      
              
    • Customers enter from the right of every bar and walk slowly toward the kegs on the left. Stop them by sliding full mugs at them.
      • 
              
    • Stand at the kegs (far left of any bar). Hold Space to fill, release to sling. The mug rides the bar to the right until a customer drinks it.
      • 
              
    • Each bar has its own pitch — bar 1 (top) is highest, bar 4 (bottom) is lowest. Stereo tells you how far along the bar a sound is.
      • 
              
    • Customers fling their empty mugs back when they finish. Walk right to catch the empty before it shatters on the floor at the kegs.
      • 
              
    • Tips appear sometimes. Run over a tip for a bonus and a brief floor show — customers freeze while the music plays.
      • 
            
    
            
    Three Ways to Lose a Life
    
            
      
              
    • A customer reaches the kegs.
      • 
              
    • An empty mug slides past the kegs and shatters.
      • 
              
    • A full mug reaches the door with no customer to catch it.
      • 
            
    
            
    Three lives. Empty the bar to clear the level. Each cycle of four bars (saloon, disco, stadium, yacht club) raises the difficulty.
