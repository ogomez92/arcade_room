How to play

Goal: Never stop. If your car ever comes to a halt, the game ends.

  • You only steer. Left/Right arrows or A/D. There are no accelerator or brake keys — speed and braking happen to you, not by you.
  • Speed cones (soft sonar bleep) give you a few seconds of acceleration when you collect them. The first cones each lap give you the most boost; later ones give less and less. You'll hear them panned where they sit on the road.
  • Fuel cans (low metallic clank) refill your tank when you collect them. Without fuel you can't accelerate, even from cones.
  • Item boxes (twinkly arpeggio) give you a random inventory item — Boost, Shield, or Fuel pack. The choice is rolled at pickup time.
  • Hazards (two-tone alarm) are wide obstacles. Crash into one and you lose ~28% of your speed instantly plus brake for ~1 second. Crashing at very low speed can stall you.
  • Your car has gears. There is no speed limit. Higher speeds burn more fuel and make crashes more punishing — balance ambition against survival.
  • Going off the road slows you down. As you drift toward the edge you'll hear warning beeps that get faster the closer you are to leaving the road.
  • Each gear change is announced on screen and to your screen reader.

Audio is top-down: the listener always faces up the road. Sounds to the left of the road are heard on your left, regardless of how you're steering. Try to keep your car centered in the audio field.

Status hotkeys

While driving, press these keys to hear the current value announced. Each key replaces the browser's default action.

  • F1 Speed in kilometers per hour
  • F2 Fuel remaining
  • F3 Current gear
  • F4 Distance driven
  • F5 Time alive
  • F6 Pickups and crashes (speed cones, fuel cans, crashes)
  • F7 Everything at once
  • G Use Boost (3-second emergency acceleration). Says "no boosts" if you have none.
  • I Inventory readout — what you're holding right now.

Inventory items

Item boxes randomly give you one of these. Some auto-fire, some are manual.

  • Boost — manual. Press G to add 3 seconds of acceleration.
  • Shield — auto. Absorbs the next hazard hit instead of letting it slow you down.
  • Fuel pack — auto. When fuel drops below 20%, instantly refills to 100%.

Pause / back to menu: Escape.

Learn the sounds

Focus or click each item to hear it. Press Escape or use the Back button to return to the menu.
