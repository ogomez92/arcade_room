      Orientation test

      How to play

      • Goal: collect every coin to clear the level.
      • Arrow keys roll the robot. Hold two for a diagonal.
      • Centre a coin's sound left-to-right to line up its column, then move until it is loudest to grab it.
      • Sounds behind you (south) are muffled and lower.
      • Avoid 1O1 bot and the rocket. A centred, loud enemy is right on top of you.
      • Space ends a level early when two or fewer coins remain.
      • E neutralizer, C collector, W wall fusion, S oil slick.
      • Enter coins and health, Shift score and level, I inventory, T time, H high score.
      • M toggles hearing one coin or all. 1-5 set how many coins to track. P pauses.
